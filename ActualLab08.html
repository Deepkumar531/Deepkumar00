Sir Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is a British Formula 1 driver and seven-time World Champion, widely considered one of the greatest drivers in the history of the sport. With over 100 Grand Prix wins, he holds the record for the most career victories, pole positions, and podium finishes in Formula 1.

Hamilton made his F1 debut in 2007 and quickly rose to prominence, winning his first title in 2008 with McLaren. He joined Mercedes in 2013, where he cemented his dominance with six additional championships.

Beyond racing, Hamilton is a passionate advocate for racial equality, environmental sustainability, and diversity in motorsport. He was knighted in 2021 for his achievements and activism and is also involved in fashion, music, and charitable work.
