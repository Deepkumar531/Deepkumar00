Top 5 F1 Drivers of All Time

Lewis Hamilton

7-time World Champion with 103 Grand Prix wins. A modern F1 legend.

Michael Schumacher

7-time World Champion and Ferrari icon with unmatched dominance in the early 2000s.

Juan Manuel Fangio

5-time World Champion who set the benchmark for excellence in the 1950s.

Alain Prost

4-time World Champion and known as "The Professor" for his strategic driving.

Ayrton Senna

3-time World Champion known for his incredible skill in wet conditions.
